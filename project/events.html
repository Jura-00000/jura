Incoming Events

Back to School

Date: October 15th, 2024

Location: San Pascual, Tarlac City

The youth are almost back to school. You are invited to attend and provide items such as notebooks, pencils, backpacks, and many others to children who need them the most. Empower these students and encourage the success of their schooling by being a volunteer or a donor in this cause.

Feeding Program for Children

Date: November 5, 2024

Location: Cut-Cut II, Tarlac City

We will host a community feeding program for children in need, providing them with nutritious meals and snacks. Volunteers are welcome to help with food preparation, serving, and engaging with the children during the event. This program is crucial for ensuring the health and well-being of the children we support.

Book Drive

Date: November 20, 2024

Location: Victoria Public Library, Tarlac

Join us in collecting books for underprivileged children. We aim to foster a love for reading and learning by providing children with access to quality educational materials. You can donate new or gently used books or volunteer to organize and distribute them during the event.

Holiday Gift-Giving

Date: December 18, 2024

Location: Barangay Hall, San Roque, Tarlac

Celebrate the holiday season by spreading joy to children in need. Help us distribute toys, clothes, and other gifts to bring smiles to their faces. Volunteers and donors are welcome to make this event a success and create a memorable experience for the kids.

Community Health Fair

Date: January 12, 2025

Location: Tarlac Provincial Hospital Grounds

Be part of our health initiative aimed at providing free medical check-ups, dental services, and health education for children and families in the community. We are looking for medical professionals and general volunteers to help during the event.

Sports for Smiles

Date: February 10, 2025

Location: Tarlac City Sports Complex

Encourage children to lead active and healthy lives by participating in our sports day. Activities include basketball, soccer, and fun relay games. Volunteers can help organize games or sponsor sports equipment to support the children.

Earth Day Clean-Up

Date: April 22, 2025

Location: San Miguel Riverbank, Tarlac

Teach children the value of environmental stewardship by joining our Earth Day river clean-up activity. Together, we will clean and beautify the riverbanks and educate children about caring for our planet. Volunteers are encouraged to bring gloves, trash bags, and other clean-up materials.
